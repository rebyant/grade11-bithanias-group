how to make burger?

1.Start with good meat. Get a butcher to grind chuck beef for you with a 15% fat content.

2.Place the ground beef in a bowl.

3.Roughly chop the onion and garlic. Put them in a bowl and mix them together until combined.

4.Add the egg yolk. Season with salt and pepper and mix everything together.

5. Mix it all together.

6. Create the burgers.
7. Arrange the burgers on a plate.

8. Choose your cooking method

9.While the burgers are cooking, prepare the toppings

10. Cover each with the top half of its bun, and enjoy

sandwich

Gather all the ingredients you need for your sandwich. Lay them out on a clean surface such as your kitchen counter or island. Take two slices of bread. You can use white, whole wheat, multi-grain, or any type you prefer. On one or both slices of bread, spread your desired condiments. Common choices include mayonnaise, mustard, butter, or ketchup. You can also get creative with other spreads. Add vegetables of your choice on one of the bread slices. Add your chosen meat if you want to have a non-vegetarian bite. You can use 2-4 slices, depending on your preference. Overlapping them evenly on the bread works well. To add creaminess and extra flavour, you can add your preferred type of cheese. If you like, you can cut your sandwich in half diagonally for a classic presentation. Or use an electric sandwich maker or grill to make it more delicious. Voila! Your sandwich is ready to eat!
